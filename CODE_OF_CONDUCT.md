# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and focus on technical outcomes.
- Welcome feedback and acknowledge mistakes.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, threats, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks or sustained disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private information without consent.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, issues, and contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

Report incidents to project maintainers through repository contact channels.

This document is intentionally concise for v1 and may evolve with the community.
